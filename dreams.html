My Dreams

Goals I'm chasing and futures I'm building.

Short-Term 1 Year

I want to improve my basic knowledge in coding and computer skills. I will practice regularly, ask questions when I don’t understand something, and study harder to strengthen my foundation. I also want to build confidence in myself and stop doubting my abilities.

Mid-Term 2-3 Years

I aim to become more comfortable and skilled in programming. I want to complete projects successfully, pass my subjects with good grades, and gain practical experience. I also hope to develop problem-solving skills and become more independent in doing coding tasks.

Long-Term 4 Years

I want to graduate from this course and find a stable job related to my field. I want to support my family financially and give back to them for their sacrifices and trust in me. I also hope to continue learning and improving my skills to become successful in my career.

Dream Idea Someday

My dream is to finish this course even though I feel weak in terms of coding and computers. I will continue to pursue this course not only for myself, but also for my family who believes in me. I want to prove to them and to myself that I can overcome my weaknesses and succeed.
